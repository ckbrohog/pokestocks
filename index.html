Restock Patterns
⚡
Dollar General is the #1 hidden alpha. Resellers almost always skip it. Rural and suburban stores often have loose packs untouched for weeks at true MSRP ($3.99–$4.99). In-person only — online is unreliable.
ALPHA
🟣
Pokémon Center has no fixed schedule. Drops happen Mon–Fri, most commonly between 10AM–3PM ET. No weekend drops. Virtual queue activates for hot products (Prismatic Evolutions, new set ETBs). Sells out in minutes. Use TrackaLacker for fastest alerts.
PC INTEL
💡
Best Buy: Tue & Thu mornings. Check the app by 8–9AM ET before heading in — app inventory updates before shelves are stocked. Use "In-Store Pickup" filter to confirm local availability.
PATTERN
🎯
Target: Use the app's "In Stock Near You" filter before driving. Online: weekday mornings ET. In-store: Tue–Thu most common but varies by your local store's vendor merchandiser schedule.
PATTERN
🛒
Walmart online often has MSRP stock when local stores are bare. Always filter "Sold & Shipped by Walmart" — third-party listings are heavily marked up.
PATTERN
🟤
Kohl's is slept on. Far less competition than Target or Best Buy. ETBs and gift sets sit on shelves much longer. Check weekly.
ALPHA
⚠️
New set launches cause 2–4 week sellout waves. ETBs and Booster Bundles go first — within 24–48hrs of release. PC-exclusive ETBs add 2 extra packs and a stamped promo vs. retail ETBs.
MARKET
🕗
Best in-store window: weekday mornings 8–10AM, right after overnight truck deliveries are stocked. By Friday afternoon, shelves are usually cleared.
